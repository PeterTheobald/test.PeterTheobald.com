Climbing Amatitlan Guatemala

2016-07-07
personal: rock climbing, travel, videos

Video from a rock climbing trip to Amatitlán, Guatemala…

On a trip to Antigua Guatemala, I went with the great guides at Old Town Outfitters to a lesser known climbing spot above Lago Amatitlán, less than an hour south of Antigua. The heart of rock climbing in Guatemala is far from Antigua near Quetzaltenango (Xela). They were trying out a new drone so I got some great video:

Edited down from lots of boring footage to 3 minutes with LightWorks fantastic free professional level video editor. Music is ‘Platoon’ by Jungle.
