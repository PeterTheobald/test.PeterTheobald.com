Rebalance a Portfolio of Cryptocurrencies by Market Cap

2017-09-15
javascript, bitcoin, cryptocurrency, programming

This page will help you keep a “BUY AND HOLD” (HODL) portfolio of cryptocurrency coins balanced by market cap.

The idea is a few altcoins will grow and thrive but most will fade away. Read through descriptions of the altcoins, the technology and teams behind them, and pick the ones that you think have something new and unique to contribute to the cryptocurrency and blockchain ecosystem. For a gauge of how solid an altcoin is, look at the coin’s market cap and trading volume. Is there an active market in the coin or is it asleep with low value and low volume?

How much of each coin should you buy? A reasonable plan is to split your portfolio in proportion to each coin’s market cap. For example, if you included Bitcoin and Ethereum, then BTC would represent ≈ 52% and ETH ≈ 22% of your portfolio and so on for the smaller altcoins. Then once a week or once a month, use this page to auto-calculate how much of each coin to buy to rebalance your cryptocoin portfolio. Doing this will automatically dollar-cost-average your purchases and move your holdings towards coins that are gaining success.

To use this, simply copy/paste a list of your holdings in the box and enter the total amount of USD money you want to add to your portfolio. The page will fetch the current value and market cap of each coin from CoinMarketCap.com and calculate what you need to buy. If you are just starting or adding new altcoins you can list the coin codes with 0 amounts. The program will find the coin code and an amount and ignore account numbers or other irrelevant text. Never type or paste your private keys anywhere on the Internet!
